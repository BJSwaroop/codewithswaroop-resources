ROUND-WISE PREPARATION
1

Aptitude Round

Most mass-hiring companies (TCS, Infosys, Wipro) start with aptitude. Focus areas:

  • Quantitative: Percentages, Profit/Loss, Time & Work, Probability
  • Logical: Puzzles, Seating Arrangement, Blood Relations, Coding-Decoding
  • Verbal: Reading Comprehension, Sentence Correction, Para Jumbles
Best practice platform: IndiaBix + PrepInsta (company-specific question papers available)
2

Technical Round

Core CS concepts every company asks:

  • OOPs: Inheritance, Polymorphism, Abstraction, Encapsulation — with examples
  • DBMS: SQL queries (JOIN, GROUP BY), Normalization, ACID properties
  • OS: Process vs Thread, Deadlock, Paging, Scheduling algorithms
  • CN: TCP vs UDP, OSI Model, HTTP vs HTTPS, DNS
Know concepts + examples
3

Coding Round

Difficulty varies by company. Here's what to expect:

  • TCS: Easy-Medium (Arrays, Strings, Basic Math)
  • Infosys: Medium (Sorting, Searching, Recursion)
  • Wipro: Easy (Pattern printing, String manipulation)
  • Cognizant: Easy-Medium (Arrays, HashMap, Basic DP)
  • Accenture: Easy (Logical implementation, String ops)
LeetCode Easy + Medium is enough
4

HR Round

Top questions asked in every HR round:

  • "Tell me about yourself" — 2 min answer: education → skills → projects → goal
  • "Why this company?" — Research their recent product/initiative
  • "Strengths & weaknesses" — Be genuine, show self-awareness
  • "Where do you see yourself in 5 years?" — Growth within the company
  • "Any questions for us?" — ALWAYS ask about team, learning, culture

COMPANY-SPECIFIC TIPS

TCS

  • TCS NQT is the gateway — focus on quantitative + coding
  • Ninja role: 3.36 LPA | Digital role: 7 LPA | Prime role: 9 LPA
  • Higher scores in NQT = better role offered

Infosys

  • InfyTQ platform for practice — use it
  • Specialist Programmer (SP) role pays ₹9.5 LPA — needs strong DSA
  • Certification in Java/Python from InfyTQ = bonus points

Wipro

  • WILP program for freshers — lower pay but decent learning
  • Elite NLTH test: harder, but ₹6.5 LPA vs regular ₹3.5 LPA
  • Focus on aptitude — Wipro aptitude is harder than coding

Cognizant & Accenture

  • Cognizant GenC: ₹4 LPA (Next: ₹6.75 LPA, Elevate: ₹9 LPA)
  • Accenture: ASE role ₹4.5 LPA — focus on cognitive + English
  • Both value communication skills heavily in HR round
